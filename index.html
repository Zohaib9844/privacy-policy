Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our Student Productivity System handles your data when you use our application with Google Calendar integration.

Information We Collect

When you use our application, we access:

  • Your Google Calendar events and schedules
  • Basic profile information from your Google account
  • Email address for authentication

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display and manage your calendar events
  • Create and modify calendar events on your behalf
  • Authenticate you with Google's services

Data Storage and Security

We do not store any of your Google Calendar data on our servers. All interactions are performed directly between our application and Google's servers using secure protocols.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Calendar API services. Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
